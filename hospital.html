ABOUT THE IMMANUEL HEALTHCARE GROUP

The Immanuel Healthcare Group is renowned at home and abroad for medical excellence and attracts world-class Specialist Doctors and Surgeons. Founded in 1997, Immanuel Healthcare Group has grown over the years with numerous facilities spread across Nigeria with its flagship hospital located in Lagos Island.The Immanuel Hospital is a leading international healthcare provider in West Africa having been the first independent hospital in Nigeria to be awarded full international accreditation on quality by COHSASA, an international healthcare accreditation body based in South Africa. The Immanuel hospital has recently been re-accredited for another 3 year (till 2021) following her outstanding success in an audit carried out in June 2018.

INTERNATIONAL ACCREDITATION AWARD ON QUALITY

First independent hospital in Nigeria to receive the COHOSA (Council for Hospital Services Accreditation of Southern Africa) Award. Immanuel Hospital has consistently won awards such as NHEA Private Healthcare Provider for Nigeria in year 2014,2015 and 2016
